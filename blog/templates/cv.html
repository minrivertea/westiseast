I build websites - hand crafted code, modern design, and the experience to guide your website from idea to completion. Like these ones:


  • www.minrivertea.com
    An online store selling Chinese tea to the UK. The site is built in Django, and integrates with Paypal through a custom built shopping cart. As well as tight integration with Google Analytics to track usage, goals and ecommerce statistics, the site also has active A/B testing running, constantly improving the design and features through real-time user testing experiments.

  • www.laowailai.com
    A social website for foreigners in Fuzhou – this site has a public news feed for foreigners to keep up with events and info from Fuzhou, and a 'places' directory to find and review restaurants, cafes, museums, activities and tourist spots. It's integrated with Baidu Maps (like Google, but in China) for geo-location and mapping interfaces.

  • www.wesourcefactories.com
    An online store for a Fuzhou based decorations company. They design and manufacture wall decorations, and then sell to trade/individuals via their website. The site features shopping basket and payments mechanisms, as well as basic content management features to allow the non-technical founders to effectively manage their products.

  • www.chinachristmascards.com
    An adventurous project for a customer selling Christmas cards in China. The design called for quick and simple ordering of Christmas cards for foreigners located in China.

  • www.wheninchina.com
    A simple news aggregator and listing for expats living in China.

  • www.westiseast.co.uk
    This blog - this site is a technical playground for new techniques, and a place where I haphazardly record my experiences in China in words or pictures.

  • www.cloudclassy.com
    An online Bayesian classifier tool, documentation and API. I haven't got a clue how this tool works, or what it does (the science is beyond me), but I was asked to make it look pretty and readable. So there it is.

  • www.londonfrockexchange.com
    A dress exchange business based in London - I worked with LFE on a small number of technical changes to their website to put in place an effective credits system for swapping dresses, and supported their hosting migration.

  • www.kwissle.com
    A fun startup venture, and technical showcase of real-time web sockets technology, I am currently designing a user interface for this online quiz battle game.
